The Story of Le Château

Nestled in the heart of the city, Le Château has been serving exceptional cuisine for over three decades. Our commitment to excellence begins with the finest ingredients, sourced from local artisans and international purveyors.

Chef Antoine Dubois brings his passion for French gastronomy to every dish, creating an unforgettable dining experience that honors tradition while embracing innovation.
